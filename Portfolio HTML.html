ProAge
High-Protein Yogurt Brand for Seniors (60+)
Created and developed ProAge as an original food brand targeting the UK senior nutrition market. Identified a clear gap — no UK yogurt was designed exclusively for adults aged 60+ with muscle health benefits.
Designed a product with 12g protein, vitamin D, and calcium per serving. Developed full brand identity, competitor analysis, SWOT assessment, HACCP-based food safety controls, and FIR 2011-compliant labelling with allergen declarations and nutritional claims. Product Development
PROMUFF
High-Protein, Low-Sugar Functional Muffin
Inspired by real customer feedback at Gail's Bakery, where consumers avoided traditional muffins due to 42g sugar per serving (vs. WHO's 25g daily recommendation). Developed PROMUFF as a healthier alternative using erythritol and stevia.
Conducted four iterative weekly development cycles, optimising formulations with whey protein, Greek yoghurt, chia seeds, and almond flour. Applied sensory evaluation, nutritional analysis, and food safety hazard identification. Designed MAP packaging (60% CO₂, 40% N₂) achieving 90-day shelf life.
